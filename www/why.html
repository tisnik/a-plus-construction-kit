A+ Construction Kit

    Why the name "A+ Construction Kit"? was choosed

    This name is based on A-0 System which was an early tool to help developers to get their work done. It was created by Grace Hopper (yes, that Grace Hopper!) during 1951 and 1952 for the UNIVAC I electronic computer. The A-0 functioned more as a loader or linker than the modern notion of a compiler. And A+ Construction Kit is too a tool to help developers to get their work done (and it's also not a standard compiler).
